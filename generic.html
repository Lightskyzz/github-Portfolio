About me

Learn a little bit more about me and what makes me a skilled and knowledgable as a Data analyst.

My name is Shaheer Hussain and....

I’m a data analyst apprentice at the Medicines and Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency, working with the data science team to deliver reports and dashboards that provide actionable insights. My role supports stakeholders in making data-driven decisions to achieve key objectives and improve outcomes. Currently, I’m studying Data Analysis at ADA, the National College for Digital Skills, where I’m developing expertise in areas like Python for data analysis and data visualization with Power BI. I’m on track to complete my Level 6 degree apprenticeship in Digital and Technology Solutions, graduating with a BSc (Hons) by the end of 2025..

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new skills and understanding how things work—from building mechanical keyboards to staying active. At 20, I’m always ready to learn and take on new challenges that deepen my knowledge and skills.
